Who we are

Canaryhotels is a hotel chain founded in 2021 as a continuation of another tourist and hotel organization born in 2000, directed both by the same people, dedicated to Tourism, Hospitality and Restoration Our philosophy is based on offering an excellent relationship quality-price in services, following strict professionalism criteria. Comfort and quality of service are guaranteed in all our hotels since since its inception, the company has been committed to quality in all its variables, offering the best and most dignified possible to its clients, preparing and caring for the most efficient and professional staff, maintaining the facilities at the level that the client expects to find in a hotel and innovating permanently at all possible levels of the service ladder offered.
